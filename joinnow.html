Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Thalexa

What is Thalexa really?

Thalexa is a 0% commission freelance marketplace designed to help freelancers and clients connect directly. Unlike other platforms, you keep 100% of your earnings, and clients get secure project handling and post-project support. It’s perfect for students, beginners, and professionals looking for reliable freelance opportunities.

What makes Thalexa different?

0% commission, secure payments, free post-project support, affordable plans from ₹99, skill-building community, and priority access to high-budget projects.

Is my money safe?

Yes, completely. For Professional (₹649) and Elite (₹899) freelancers, clients must deposit a security amount before the project starts. This ensures you always get paid for your work, and no scams happen.

Who should join Thalexa?

Students start with ₹99 Basic, growing freelancers choose ₹649 Professional, full-timers go for ₹899 Elite, and clients join for ₹899 to get reliable freelancers with 2 months of support.

How is my project and payment secured as a freelancer?

With the Professional and Elite plans, the client must deposit a security amount before work begins. This guarantees your payment. If any issue arises, the Thalexa team provides priority support to resolve disputes quickly.

Do clients get after-project support?

Clients with a Client Membership (₹899 for 3 months) get 2 months of after-project support from freelancers. This ensures that if anything breaks or needs updates, the client is covered without extra costs.

I don't have any skills. Can I still freelance on Thalexa?

You can join with the Basic plan (₹99) and access free learning resources through Thalexa Learn. Start with beginner-friendly gigs like: Data entry Simple design Social media tasks As you grow, upgrade to the Professional or Elite plan for better projects and earnings.
